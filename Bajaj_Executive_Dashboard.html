What the numbers are saying

Finding 01 · Category mix

Furniture drives 29% of sales but only 6% of profit — a 2.2% margin vs. 11.2% company-wide. Technology, a similar sales share, delivers 52% of profit.

Finding 02 · Growth quality

2020 sales grew 77% over 2019, but profit grew only 14%. Margin fell from 14.5% to 9.3% — growth is currently costing efficiency.

Finding 03 · Returns risk

West's 9.9% return rate is really a California problem (12.4% on 1,189 orders). The rest of the West region returns in line with the country.
